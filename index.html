üß† Why Wikidata?

Wikidata is a central hub of structured, open, and multilingual knowledge. It powers Wikipedia, search engines, and AI systems.

  • Supports knowledge graphs and SPARQL queries
  • Promotes open knowledge for underrepresented cultures
  • Follows RDF and semantic web standards
  • Connects music with people, genres, instruments, and events

üåç Why These Countries?

We chose Uzbekistan, Morocco, and Tunisia for both personal and academic reasons.

  • Each team member represents one of these countries
  • All three have rich musical heritage (e.g. Shashmaqam, Andalusi music, Malouf)
  • These cultures are underrepresented in Wikidata
  • We explore both Central Asian and North African perspectives
